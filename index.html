About Us

Welcome to SISI PINEAPPLE, home to premium pineapples from Sipahutar, North Tapanuli. We are a family business dedicated to growing and exporting the best pineapples cultivated in fertile soil and ideal climate at an altitude of 1,000-1,500 meters above sea level.

Sipahutar Pineapples are widely known for their unmatched sweetness and soft, juicy texture. Each pineapple is picked at the peak of its freshness, ensuring the best quality for our customers worldwide. With years of experience, we ensure that every step in the process, from planting to shipping, is carried out to the highest standards.

At SISI PINEAPPLE, we focus not only on product quality but also on sustainable farming practices. We believe that preserving the environment is key to a better future. Therefore, we use environmentally friendly and sustainable farming methods.

We are proud to be part of the Sipahutar community, known for having the largest pineapple plantations in the world. Every family in this village owns extensive pineapple farms, and we work together to promote Sipahutar pineapples to the international market. With a production of around 18 tons per day, we are ready to meet the high-quality pineapple needs of our customers.

Thank you for choosing SISI PINEAPPLE. We are committed to delivering the best from our farms directly to your table.
