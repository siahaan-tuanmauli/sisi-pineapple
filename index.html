About Us

SISI PINEAPPLE is a family business in Sipahutar, North Tapanuli, that specializes in growing and exporting premium pineapples. These pineapples are known for their exceptional sweetness and juicy texture, thanks to the fertile soil and ideal climate at 1,000-1,500 meters above sea level. The company ensures the highest quality by picking each pineapple at its peak freshness. Furthermore, SISI PINEAPPLE takes pride in using sustainable agricultural practices, aiming to preserve the environment for a better future. The business is part of the Sipahutar community, which is famous for having the largest pineapple plantation in the world. With a production rate of approximately 18 tons per day, SISI PINEAPPLE can meet the demand for high-quality pineapples. The company is dedicated to providing the best products to customers, straight from their garden to the customers' tables.

Products

Sipahutar Fresh Pineapples from SISI PINEAPPLE are the best choice for those seeking high-quality pineapples with natural sweetness and a soft texture. Grown in fertile soil and ideal climate at an altitude of 1,000-1,500 meters above sea level, our pineapples are picked at the peak of their freshness to ensure the best quality.
